Query Language Reference

Indicator Reference:

  • macd(long-term moving average, short-term moving average)
  • macd_signal(long-term moving average, short-term moving average, period)
  • sma(period)
  • ema(period)
  • rsi(period)
  • stochastic(period)
  • stochastic_signal(period, smoothing)
  • full_stochastic(period, moving average)
  • full_stochastic_signal(period, moving average, smoothing)
  • price

Transforms

You can place transforms after any of the non-signal indicators to perform one of the following: ema, sma, macd, macd_signal. They take the same parameters as the indicator varieties.

e.g. rsi(14)->ema(7) gives the 7-day ema of the rsi(14)
